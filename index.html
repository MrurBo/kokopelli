About Kokopelli

Kokopelli is a small project based in Dublin, born from love and respect for plants. At its heart is Rapé (Hapé) — the sacred Amazonian snuff. Each blend comes directly from indigenous tribes and is prepared by hand, one order at a time, so what you receive is always fresh and treated with care.

Alongside Rapé, we also share Lavender oil from the French Alps — a calming, grounding ally that brings a touch of European soil into our offering.

The name Kokopelli comes from an ancient figure known as a bringer of music, growth, and transformation. For us, it’s a reminder that plants are companions: they help us ground, find clarity, and reconnect with ourselves and the world around us.

Our approach

We keep things simple, guided by three values:

  • Respect for tradition — Rapé is medicine with deep Amazonian roots. We honour these lineages and share with reverence.
  • Clarity and honesty — We are open about our sourcing, ingredients, and intentions.
  • Sustainability — Using minimal packaging and preparing only what is needed, freshly for each order.

Why Rapé?

Rapé is more than a product — it is a sacred tool. Traditionally used to clear the mind, align the spirit, and ground the body, it holds a special place in prayer and ritual. Each blend we share is received from tribes and packaged fresh by hand for you.

And Lavender oil

Lavender oil is our gentle European counterpart. Distilled in the French Alps, it carries a calming and grounding presence — a simple way to invite stillness and balance into daily life.

Together, Rapé and Lavender oil form the essence of Kokopelli: plant allies for clarity, grounding, and connection.

Note: All items are for ritual and aromatic use only. Nothing shared here is intended as medical advice or a substitute for professional care.
